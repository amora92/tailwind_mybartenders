## Target Audience

### Primary Audiences

#### 1. Engaged Couples (Wedding Market)
- **Age**: 25-40
- **Income**: £40,000+ household
- **Behaviour**: Planning 12-18 months ahead
- **Pain Points**: Want unique entertainment, worried about bar quality
- **Where to Find**: Wedding blogs, Pinterest, Instagram, wedding fairs

#### 2. Corporate Event Planners
- **Role**: HR, Marketing, Office Managers
- **Company Size**: 50-500 employees
- **Events**: Christmas parties, summer BBQs, team building
- **Pain Points**: Need reliable, professional service
- **Where to Find**: LinkedIn, industry events, Google search

#### 3. Private Party Hosts
- **Age**: 30-55
- **Occasions**: Milestone birthdays, anniversaries, garden parties
- **Income**: £50,000+ household
- **Pain Points**: Want to impress guests without the stress
- **Where to Find**: Facebook, Instagram, local Facebook groups

#### 4. Event Planners & Venues
- **Type**: Professional event planners, venue coordinators
- **Need**: Reliable vendor partnerships
- **Value**: Repeat business, referrals
- **Where to Find**: Networking events, LinkedIn, industry directories

### Audience Personas

**"Wedding Wendy"**
- 28 years old, engaged
- Works in marketing
- Planning a rustic barn wedding
- Budget: £30,000-£50,000
- Wants unique cocktail menu matching colour scheme
- Active on Pinterest and Instagram

**"Corporate Chris"**
- 42 years old, Office Manager
- Organising company summer party for 150 people
- Budget: £5,000-£10,000
- Needs professional, reliable service
- Values clear pricing and good communication

**"Party Pete"**
- 45 years old, celebrating 50th birthday
- Hosting garden party for 60 guests
- Budget: £2,000-£4,000
- Wants to impress without the hassle
- Found you via Google search
